How it started

A problem every
pet parent knows.

Finding a trustworthy groomer. Booking a last-minute vet. Knowing your dog is safe while you're at work. For millions of Indian pet owners, these aren't small inconveniences — they're daily stressors.

Sai Kolanupaka experienced this firsthand. After struggling to find reliable, verified pet care in Hyderabad, he realised the gap wasn't just local — it was a nationwide problem with no real solution. No single platform combined quality professionals, GPS live tracking, digital health records, and seamless booking.

So he built one. PETclub launched with a clear mission: make professional pet care as easy as ordering food online — fast, transparent, and completely trustworthy.

What we stand for

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from how we verify professionals to how we handle a complaint at midnight.

🔒
Trust above everything

Every professional on PETclub goes through a manual 48-hour review — ID verification, certifications, background checks. If we wouldn't trust them with our own pet, they don't get on the platform.

📍
Radical transparency

Live GPS tracking on every walk and visit. Real-time updates sent to you. Digital health records that don't disappear. You always know exactly what's happening with your pet.

🤝
Pros are partners

We don't treat professionals as gig workers. They're partners. Fair payouts, no hidden fees, weekly settlements, and the tools to grow their business — not just fill our platform.

🐶
The pet always comes first

Every product decision starts with one question: does this make life better for the pet? Not just more convenient for the owner, not just more profitable for us — better for the animal.

⚡
Speed with care

Pet emergencies don't wait. We've built our dispatch system to match a professional within minutes, not hours. Fast doesn't mean careless — it means we've already done the hard work of verification so you don't have to.

🌱
Built to last

We're not chasing quick growth metrics. We're building a brand that pet owners recommend to their friends for years — one honest, reliable, life-changing experience at a time.

Our journey

From idea to platform

2024 — The Idea
A problem worth solving

After one too many bad grooming experiences and a near-miss with an unverified vet, Sai decides the pet care market in India needed a complete rebuild from the ground up.

💡
🛠️
Early 2025 — Building
Platform takes shape

The core platform is built: OTP-verified onboarding, professional verification pipeline, GPS live tracking, digital health records, and the booking engine. Eshwar joins as CEO to lead operations and growth.

Mid 2025 — Launch
Hyderabad goes live

PETclub launches in Hyderabad with grooming, training, and vet care. First 500 bookings in 30 days. Word-of-mouth growth from pet owners who finally found something that worked.

🚀
🦮
2025 — Expanding
Walker & Boarding go live

Dog walking with GPS tracking and cage-free pet boarding added. The platform expands to 100+ cities. Professional network crosses 1,200 verified pros. USA pilot begins.

2026 & Beyond
Building India's pet care OS

Loyalty programme, smart health passport, and automated vet reminders launch. The mission remains the same: every pet, every city, every day — cared for by someone who's been verified to deserve that trust.
