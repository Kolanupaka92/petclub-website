About PETclub

Approved boilerplate

Please use this approved description when writing about us.

PETclub is India's fastest-growing verified pet care platform, connecting pet owners with trusted groomers, trainers, veterinarians, walkers, and boarders through a single mobile-first app. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Hyderabad, PETclub is building the infrastructure for professional pet care in India — combining real-time booking, digital health records, GPS-tracked walks, and verified professional profiles. The platform serves over 50,000 pets across 100+ cities, with a network of more than 1,200 background-checked professionals. PETclub is founded by Sai Kolanupaka and led by CEO Eshwar Kolanupaka.
