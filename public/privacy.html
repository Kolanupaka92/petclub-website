1 Who We Are

PETclub operates the pet services marketplace at mypetclub.app and app.mypetclub.app. For the purposes of data protection law, PETclub is the data controller (India) / business (US) for personal data collected through the App.

We serve customers in India and the United States. Our App connects pet owners with independent grooming, training, and veterinary professionals for dogs, cats, and other domestic pets.

Contact our privacy team: privacy@mypetclub.app

4 Legal Basis for Processing

4.1 India — DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, we process your personal data on the following grounds:

  • Consent — you provide consent when you create an account and accept these Terms. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 7).
  • Legitimate uses — processing necessary for providing requested services, processing payments, and complying with legal obligations.
  • Legal obligation — where required by Indian law, court order, or government authority.

4.2 United States

For US residents, we comply with applicable state privacy laws including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar statutes. We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA. US residents have the rights described in Section 7.

We process your data based on: (a) performance of a contract (providing the services you request); (b) legitimate interests (platform security, analytics); and (c) legal obligation.

8 Children's Privacy

PETclub is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data to PETclub, please contact us at privacy@mypetclub.app and we will delete such data promptly.

Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account (see Terms of Service, Section 2).

10 Data Security

PETclub implements industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data fields in our database are encrypted
  • Access controls: Role-based access ensures team members only access data needed for their function. Admin-only data (e.g., Professional ID verifications) is inaccessible to all non-admin systems and personnel
  • Authentication: Firebase Phone OTP and Email OTP — no passwords stored
  • Penetration testing: Periodic third-party security reviews
  • Audit logging: All access to sensitive data is logged with timestamps

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@mypetclub.app.

Security incident notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by the DPDP Act 2023 (India) and applicable US state breach notification laws, within the mandated timeframes.

11 International Data Transfers

PETclub's primary infrastructure is hosted on servers in India and the United States. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in either or both jurisdictions.

When transferring personal data from India to the US (e.g., through Firebase or Supabase), PETclub ensures appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with the DPDP Act 2023 and any cross-border transfer regulations notified by the Government of India.

For US users whose data may be stored or processed in India, we ensure equivalent data protection standards apply through contractual safeguards with our service providers.
