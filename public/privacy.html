Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Effective: May 19, 2026

PETclub India is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only what we need to deliver pet care services, and we never sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

PETclub India ("PETclub", "we", "us") operates the pet care platform at mypetclub.app and app.mypetclub.app. Our registered office is at Sahara, LB Nagar, Hyderabad – 500074, Telangana, India.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Identity

  • Mobile phone number (required for OTP authentication)
  • Email address (required for India users; optional for others)
  • Full name
  • Government-issued ID details (professionals only — Aadhar, PAN, Passport, DL)

Pet Information

  • Pet name, species, breed, age, gender
  • Health records, vaccination history, vet notes you enter

Booking & Service Data

  • Service type, date, time, location
  • Service notes and special instructions
  • Ratings and reviews you submit

Technical Data

  • FCM device token (for push notifications — stored encrypted, used only to send you notifications)
  • IP address and request metadata (for security and rate limiting)
  • Browser/device type (standard web logs)

Payment Data

  • Payment status and Razorpay order/payment IDs
  • We do not store card numbers, CVVs, or bank account details — all payment processing is handled by Razorpay

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity via OTP and issue secure session tokens
  • Match you with verified service professionals
  • Send booking confirmations, status updates, and service reminders
  • Send push notifications (only with your explicit permission)
  • Process payments securely via Razorpay
  • Review and verify professional applications (ID verification)
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Improve our platform through aggregate, anonymised analytics

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Twilio — SMS OTP delivery (twilio.com/legal/privacy)
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (resend.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — Database hosting on AWS (supabase.com/privacy) — servers in the USA
  • Firebase / Google — Push notifications via FCM (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Razorpay — Payment processing (razorpay.com/privacy)
  • Vercel — Frontend hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Railway — Backend hosting (railway.app/legal/privacy)

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With professionals: Your first name, pet details, and service address are shared with the assigned service professional to fulfil your booking.
  • With our service providers: Third-party tools listed in Section 4 — only what each service needs to function.
  • Legal compliance: If required by Indian law or court order.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Booking records are retained for 3 years for legal/tax compliance
  • OTP codes expire within 10 minutes and are never stored after use
  • FCM tokens are deleted when you log out or revoke notification permission
  • You may request deletion of your account at any time (see Section 8)

7. Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are not used — authentication is OTP-only
  • JWT tokens expire automatically and are stored only in browser localStorage
  • Government ID documents are visible only to PETclub admins and are not shared
  • Database access is restricted to our backend service via Supabase service keys

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Update inaccurate information via the app
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Notification opt-out — Revoke push notification permission in your device settings anytime

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@mypetclub.app.

9. Children's Privacy

PETclub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. We'll notify registered users of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect.
